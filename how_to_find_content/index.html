    How to Find Content¶

    Finding new or upcoming content is one of the most important skills when working with WEB-DLs. Whether you're checking what has been released, what is coming soon, or which region offers the best version, the sites below make the process straightforward.

    JustWatch – Worldwide Availability Tracking¶

    JustWatch tracks streaming availability across many regions and updates quickly.
    Use it to:

    • Identify which platform carries a title
    • Track new releases day-by-day
    • Compare availability between regions
    • Locate alternative streams for better masters or audio options

    Link:
    ➡️ https://justwatch.com/

    JustWatch-Search (All Regions Simultaneously)¶

    This tool expands on JustWatch by allowing you to search all regions at once.
    Useful for:

    • Finding earlier or exclusive regional releases
    • Discovering hidden catalog titles
    • Comparing availability across countries without switching regions manually

    Link:
    ➡️ https://nobraincellsleft.github.io/JustWatch-Search/

    Stream With VPN – Discover Hidden Regional Content¶

    Stream With VPN helps you find movies and TV shows that are available only in specific countries.
    It scans a large number of services and regions, making it useful for:

    • Locating titles not available in your home region
    • Finding early releases or exclusive regional drops
    • Discovering alternate masters offered in other countries
    • Expanding sourcing options for comparisons and WEB-DLs

    This is especially helpful when streaming platforms stagger releases or offer unique encodes by region.

    Link:
    ➡️ https://www.streamwithvpn.com/

    Releases.com – Upcoming Movies & TV Tracking¶

    Releases.com provides an easy way to track release dates for movies, TV shows, games, and more.

    Use it to:

    • Monitor upcoming premiere dates across platforms
    • Prepare automation/scripts ahead of WEB-DL drops
    • Track regional release windows
    • Get reminders for new seasons, specials, and limited releases

    A perfect companion to JustWatch when planning for future content.

    Link:
    ➡️ https://www.releases.com/

    Tracking Upcoming Releases¶

    Knowing release dates helps you prepare scripts, automation, and subtitle cleanup ahead of time.

    Reliable places to check:

    • Official platform “Coming Soon” pages
    • Press announcements
    • Studio or distributor social media
    • Trailer releases, which often indicate the release window

    WEB-DLs typically become available immediately when a title goes live on its streaming platform.

    Checking Services You Already Use¶

    If you maintain subscriptions, review:

    • The platform’s upcoming release calendar
    • Regional version differences using JustWatch
    • Whether UHD, HDR, or alternate audio mixes are available
    • If the release schedules differ across regions

    Sometimes services silently update their catalogs hours early.

    Why Regional Differences Matter¶

    Different regions may receive:

    • Higher bitrate encodes
    • Alternate crops or reframed masters
    • Unique audio and subtitle sets
    • Early releases before other regions
    • Completely different master sources

    Example:
    An Amazon title may release in Turkey or India earlier than the United States, and Netflix Japan often carries unique encodes.

    Summary¶

    Use these tools and methods to determine:

    • What has released
    • Which regions have availability
    • Where the best-quality stream originates
    • Whether HDR or UHD is available
    • Whether a title is worth comparing or archiving

    Efficient content discovery is the first step toward proper WEB-DL handling.
